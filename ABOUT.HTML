ABOUT HHA

Hang Harvest Agriculture Co,.Ltd is the a company from the northern part of Cambodia, Oddar Meanchey Province. Agriculture is the heart of Hangs business which is initially started for 13 years from collecting tapioca chip as family business and develop to become the major agricultural industrial company. Then, it was expanded to agricultural export business, which had one big project for tapioca chip warehouses, and loading port have been started in 2023. Our company is doing agricultural warehousing business as for raw material stock keeping saving for government projects and also for private sectors in the central area of Cambodia.

HISTORY OF HANG HARVEST AGRICULTURE

YEAR 2023

Hang Harvest Agriculture Co.,Ltd successfully exported tapioca chips to China.

YEAR 2022

To expand the Cambodian cassava market to China, Hangs founder had extended a warehouse in Kompong Speur province with 60300 square meters. That can stock 30000 tons of dry tapioca chip.

YEAR 2015

Established of Hang Harvest Agriculture Co., Ltd for Tapioca chips commodity export, With high capacity of export 1,100 tons/day, the company had potentially served local animal feed manufacturers.
